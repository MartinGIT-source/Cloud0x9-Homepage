Cloud0x9.

How we met

A long time ago in several houses far away, five intrepid developers were forced to work together and faced many challenges. The barely survived the hell weeks, but this incredible feat gave them a light beam of hope and, with renewed energy, they were able to carry on with the program. Now, with all their new knowledge, they are set to become the best Mob in the whole galaxy.

How we came up with our name

It was probably our biggest challenge. Many great names were suggested, like Skyward, but one thing was for sure, this Mob was destined to be in the heavens. After several polls and heated discussions, Cloud0x9 became our oficial name.
